integer, parameter, private fdtd::dp = KIND(1.d0)

Electric and Magnetic Fields.

Because of the staggering of the Yee grid, the fields are offset from each other. Therefore, Hx(m,n+1/2), Hy(m+1/2,n), and Ez(m,n) share the same index, [m, n].
